================================================================================
KNOWN LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

1. EXTRACTION ACCURACY
   - Depends on document format and structure
   - Better with well-formatted documents
   - May need manual adjustment for complex documents

2. DOCUMENT FORMAT
   - Requires structured text (not images/scans)
   - Complex layouts may not extract perfectly
   - Password-protected PDFs not supported

3. SKILL DETECTION
   - Limited to predefined skill dictionary (~50 skills)
   - May miss specialized or niche technologies
   - Can be extended with custom skill list

4. SINGLE JOB PER UPLOAD
   - One document = one job
   - Multiple jobs require multiple uploads
   - By design (focused ranking)
